// 01 — Process

A four-step loop, repeated every campaign.

  1. 01

    Planning

    Brief, narrative, content calendar and shot list — aligned with the brand's voice and quarterly goals.

  2. 02

    Capture

    On-location photography and video, directing talent, scouting light and framing for the feed and for stories.

  3. 03

    Production

    Selects, colour grading and finishing. Feed-ready 4:5 posts and vertical 9:16 stories consistent with the identity.

  4. 04

    Impact

    Read the numbers — reach, saves, engagement, attributed sales — and feed learnings back into the next plan.
